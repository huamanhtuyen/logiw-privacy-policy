How to Delete Your Data

This guide will help you delete your transportation vehicle listing data from LogiW app without deleting your entire account. Follow these 6 simple steps:

Important Note:

Once you delete your listing data, it cannot be recovered. Please ensure you have backed up any important information before proceeding.

1
Open LogiW App and Access Your Profile
Launch the LogiW application on your mobile device and tap on the profile icon (menu icon with 3 parallel lines) in the top right corner of the screen.
2
Navigate to "Posted Vehicles"
"我的车辆"，查看您所有的运输车辆发布信息。">From your profile menu, find and tap on "My Posts" -> "My Vehicles" to view all your transportation vehicle listings.
3
Select the Vehicle Listing to Delete
Browse through your vehicle listings and select the specific transportation post you want to delete by tapping on it.
4
Access Listing Options Menu
Look for the dropdown arrow (usually represented by a down arrow ↓) and tap on it to open the listing management options.
5
Select "Delete" or "Remove Listing"
From the options menu, find and tap on "Delete", "Remove Listing", or "Delete Post" option. This will initiate the deletion process.
6
Confirm Deletion
A confirmation dialog will appear asking you to confirm the deletion. Read the warning message carefully and tap "Confirm", "Delete", or "Yes" to permanently delete your transportation listing data.
