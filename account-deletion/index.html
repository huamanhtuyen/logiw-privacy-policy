How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to permanently delete your LogiW account, please follow these simple steps. Once deleted, all your data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

1
Open Profile Settings
Launch the LogiW app and navigate to your profile. Tap on the settings icon or menu option to access your account settings.
2
Access Account Management
In the settings menu, look for 'Account Management' or 'Privacy Settings'. This section contains options for managing your personal data and account preferences.
3
Find Delete Account Option
Scroll down in the account management section to find the 'Delete Account' or 'Deactivate Account' option. This is usually located at the bottom of the settings page.
4
Confirm Account Deletion
Tap on 'Delete Account' and carefully read the confirmation message. Enter your password or verify your identity as required, then confirm the deletion. Your account will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
